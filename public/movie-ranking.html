Zack's Favorite Movies

My top 3 movies of all-time

Avengers - End Game

This is my favorite Marvels movie because it incorporates all my favorite characters from the Marvel universe.

Everything Everywhere, All at Once

This movie was really trippy yet very entertaining to watch. It has a lot of psychedelic elements and incorporates an all Asian cast!

Get Out

This movie, directed by Jordan Peele, is a thriller and a societal allegory. It has a very ironic plot and kept me feeling uneasy, as it should, throughout the film.
